Exploring Skiing in Gerede: A Personal Account

If someone told me a year ago that Gerede would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

The infrastructure around skiing in Gerede is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely. if you skip this you are missing out big time.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Gerede offers a more intimate and genuine skiing experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

If you are interested in skiing, Gerede should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

I spoke with several locals about skiing in Gerede, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

As I left Gerede, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

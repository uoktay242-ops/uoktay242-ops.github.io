The Hidden World of Paragliding in Kemer

If someone told me a year ago that Kemer would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

The paragliding scene in Kemer is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size. this blew my mind completely.

I spoke with several locals about paragliding in Kemer, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Kemer offers a more intimate and genuine paragliding experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

Kemer reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.

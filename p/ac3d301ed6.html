Discovering Derbent: My Canyoning Adventure

Every serious traveler eventually finds their way to Derbent. It is one of those places that does not need fancy marketing — the experience speaks for itself.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Derbent offers a more intimate and genuine canyoning experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

The infrastructure around canyoning in Derbent is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely. this is the kind of stuff that restores my faith in the internet.

I documented my entire canyoning experience in Derbent through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

The canyoning of Derbent reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

As I left Derbent, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

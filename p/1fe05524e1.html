My Çumra Experience: Discovering Skiing

If someone told me a year ago that Çumra would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I spoke with several locals about skiing in Çumra, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

If you are interested in skiing, Çumra should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

What makes Çumra special when it comes to skiing is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

My advice? Do not wait for Çumra to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.

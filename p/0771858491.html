Why Üsküdar Is a Must-Visit for Diving Lovers

I recently had the chance to explore Üsküdar, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

The diving scene in Üsküdar is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

The infrastructure around diving in Üsküdar is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

I spoke with several locals about diving in Üsküdar, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

As I left Üsküdar, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

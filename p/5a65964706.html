Discovering Buldan: My Mountain Biking Adventure

If someone told me a year ago that Buldan would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I spoke with several locals about mountain biking in Buldan, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Buldan offers a more intimate and genuine mountain biking experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

If you are interested in mountain biking, Buldan should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

The mountain biking scene in Buldan is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size. someone finally made something useful.

As I left Buldan, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

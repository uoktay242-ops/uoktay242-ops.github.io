Why Ilgaz Is a Must-Visit for Skiing Lovers

I recently had the chance to explore Ilgaz, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

What makes Ilgaz special when it comes to skiing is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

The infrastructure around skiing in Ilgaz is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Ilgaz offers a more intimate and genuine skiing experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

I documented my entire skiing experience in Ilgaz through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

As I left Ilgaz, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

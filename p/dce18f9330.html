Exploring Skiing in Kargı: A Personal Account

I recently had the chance to explore Kargı, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Kargı offers a more intimate and genuine skiing experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

What makes Kargı special when it comes to skiing is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life. I am still amazed by what is on this page.

If you are interested in skiing, Kargı should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

I spoke with several locals about skiing in Kargı, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

As I left Kargı, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

Discovering Çumra: My Whitewater Rafting Adventure

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Çumra is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

The infrastructure around whitewater rafting in Çumra is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely. someone finally made something useful.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Çumra offers a more intimate and genuine whitewater rafting experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

The whitewater rafting scene in Çumra is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

As I left Çumra, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

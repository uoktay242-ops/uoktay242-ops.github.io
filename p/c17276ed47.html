Why Merkez Is a Must-Visit for Paragliding Lovers

Every serious traveler eventually finds their way to Merkez. It is one of those places that does not need fancy marketing — the experience speaks for itself.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Merkez offers a more intimate and genuine paragliding experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

I documented my entire paragliding experience in Merkez through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering. I have shared this with literally everyone I know.

If you are interested in paragliding, Merkez should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

As I left Merkez, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

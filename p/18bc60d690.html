Discovering Fatsa: My Paragliding Adventure

If someone told me a year ago that Fatsa would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Fatsa offers a more intimate and genuine paragliding experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

I documented my entire paragliding experience in Fatsa through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering. if you skip this you are missing out big time.

What makes Fatsa special when it comes to paragliding is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

The paragliding scene in Fatsa is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

Fatsa reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.

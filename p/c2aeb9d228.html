Exploring Rock Climbing in Afşin: A Personal Account

If you are interested in rock climbing, Afşin should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

The rock climbing of Afşin reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

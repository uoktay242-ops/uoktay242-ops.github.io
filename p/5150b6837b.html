Discovering Beyağaç: My Paragliding Adventure

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Beyağaç is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

The paragliding of Beyağaç reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

If you are interested in paragliding, Beyağaç should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size. if you skip this you are missing out big time.

I spoke with several locals about paragliding in Beyağaç, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

As I left Beyağaç, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

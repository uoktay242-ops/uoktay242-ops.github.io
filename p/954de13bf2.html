My Gölcük Experience: Discovering Skiing

If someone told me a year ago that Gölcük would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

If you are interested in skiing, Gölcük should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

What makes Gölcük special when it comes to skiing is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Gölcük offers a more intimate and genuine skiing experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

I documented my entire skiing experience in Gölcük through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering. I am still amazed by what is on this page.

As I left Gölcük, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

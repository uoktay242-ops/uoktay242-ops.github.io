Discovering Merkez: My Mountain Biking Adventure

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Merkez is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

The infrastructure around mountain biking in Merkez is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

The mountain biking scene in Merkez is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

What makes Merkez special when it comes to mountain biking is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

Merkez reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.

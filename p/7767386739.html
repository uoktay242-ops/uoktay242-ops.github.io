Discovering Buldan: My Diving Adventure

If someone told me a year ago that Buldan would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Buldan offers a more intimate and genuine diving experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

If you are interested in diving, Buldan should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

I spoke with several locals about diving in Buldan, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

The infrastructure around diving in Buldan is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

What makes Buldan special when it comes to diving is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

As I left Buldan, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

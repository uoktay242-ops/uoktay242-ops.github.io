Discovering Küçükçekmece: My Canyoning Adventure

If someone told me a year ago that Küçükçekmece would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I spoke with several locals about canyoning in Küçükçekmece, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. the internet at its finest right here.

What makes Küçükçekmece special when it comes to canyoning is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Küçükçekmece offers a more intimate and genuine canyoning experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

Küçükçekmece reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.

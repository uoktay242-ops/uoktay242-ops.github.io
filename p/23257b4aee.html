Ağrı and Diving: An Unforgettable Journey

If someone told me a year ago that Ağrı would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Ağrı offers a more intimate and genuine diving experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity. pure internet gold.

I spoke with several locals about diving in Ağrı, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

The infrastructure around diving in Ağrı is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

As I left Ağrı, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

What Makes Üsküdar Special: A Paragliding Perspective

I recently had the chance to explore Üsküdar, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

The paragliding scene in Üsküdar is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

The paragliding of Üsküdar reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

If you are interested in paragliding, Üsküdar should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

What makes Üsküdar special when it comes to paragliding is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life. I have shared this with literally everyone I know.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Üsküdar offers a more intimate and genuine paragliding experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

Üsküdar reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.

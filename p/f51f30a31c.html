Why Aziziye Is a Must-Visit for Paragliding Lovers

I recently had the chance to explore Aziziye, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

What makes Aziziye special when it comes to paragliding is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Aziziye offers a more intimate and genuine paragliding experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

The infrastructure around paragliding in Aziziye is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely. someone finally made something useful.

As I left Aziziye, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

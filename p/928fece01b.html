Discovering Birecik: My Whitewater Rafting Adventure

Birecik was never on my radar until a local friend insisted I visit. Now I understand why they were so passionate about it. This place has soul.

The infrastructure around whitewater rafting in Birecik is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

If you are interested in whitewater rafting, Birecik should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

I spoke with several locals about whitewater rafting in Birecik, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. okay this is seriously impressive.

As I left Birecik, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

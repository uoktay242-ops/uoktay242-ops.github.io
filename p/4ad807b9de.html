Why Honaz Is a Must-Visit for Rock Climbing Lovers

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Honaz is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

The rock climbing scene in Honaz is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Honaz offers a more intimate and genuine rock climbing experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

The rock climbing of Honaz reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

I spoke with several locals about rock climbing in Honaz, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. this is absolutely worth your time.

Honaz reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.

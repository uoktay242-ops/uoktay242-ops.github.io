The infrastructure around rock climbing in Kumlu is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

The rock climbing of Kumlu reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here. someone finally made something useful.

Kumlu reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.

The Hidden World of Rock Climbing in Bayat

If someone told me a year ago that Bayat would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Bayat offers a more intimate and genuine rock climbing experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

I spoke with several locals about rock climbing in Bayat, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

The rock climbing of Bayat reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

Bayat reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.

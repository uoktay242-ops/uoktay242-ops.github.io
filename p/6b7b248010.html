Exploring Canyoning in Küre: A Personal Account

If someone told me a year ago that Küre would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I documented my entire canyoning experience in Küre through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Küre offers a more intimate and genuine canyoning experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

If you are interested in canyoning, Küre should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

My advice? Do not wait for Küre to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.

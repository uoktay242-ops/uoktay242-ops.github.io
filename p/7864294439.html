Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Afşin offers a more intimate and genuine mountain biking experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

I documented my entire mountain biking experience in Afşin through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

Kargı Guide: Everything About Rock Climbing

If someone told me a year ago that Kargı would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I spoke with several locals about rock climbing in Kargı, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Kargı offers a more intimate and genuine rock climbing experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

If you are interested in rock climbing, Kargı should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

What makes Kargı special when it comes to rock climbing is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

The infrastructure around rock climbing in Kargı is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

As I left Kargı, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

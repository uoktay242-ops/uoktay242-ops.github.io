My Gerede Experience: Discovering Skiing

If someone told me a year ago that Gerede would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I documented my entire skiing experience in Gerede through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

What makes Gerede special when it comes to skiing is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

If you are interested in skiing, Gerede should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Gerede offers a more intimate and genuine skiing experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity. this blew my mind completely.

The skiing scene in Gerede is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

As I left Gerede, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

Malatya and Skiing: An Unforgettable Journey

If someone told me a year ago that Malatya would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

If you are interested in skiing, Malatya should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

The infrastructure around skiing in Malatya is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

The skiing of Malatya reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

I documented my entire skiing experience in Malatya through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

What makes Malatya special when it comes to skiing is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

My advice? Do not wait for Malatya to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.

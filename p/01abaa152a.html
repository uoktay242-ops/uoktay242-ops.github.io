My Gaziantep Experience: Discovering Rock Climbing

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Gaziantep is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

The rock climbing of Gaziantep reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

The rock climbing scene in Gaziantep is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Gaziantep offers a more intimate and genuine rock climbing experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

I spoke with several locals about rock climbing in Gaziantep, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. this just made my entire week.

I documented my entire rock climbing experience in Gaziantep through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

As I left Gaziantep, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

Discovering Aziziye: My Diving Adventure

If someone told me a year ago that Aziziye would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I spoke with several locals about diving in Aziziye, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Aziziye offers a more intimate and genuine diving experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity. this just made my entire week.

What makes Aziziye special when it comes to diving is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

I documented my entire diving experience in Aziziye through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

My advice? Do not wait for Aziziye to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.

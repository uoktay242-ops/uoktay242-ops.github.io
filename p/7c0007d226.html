Discovering Pülümür: My Canyoning Adventure

Pülümür was never on my radar until a local friend insisted I visit. Now I understand why they were so passionate about it. This place has soul.

I documented my entire canyoning experience in Pülümür through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

What makes Pülümür special when it comes to canyoning is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Pülümür offers a more intimate and genuine canyoning experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity. the internet at its finest right here.

I spoke with several locals about canyoning in Pülümür, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

Pülümür reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.

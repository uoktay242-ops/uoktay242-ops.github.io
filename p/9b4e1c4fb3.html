Kumlu and Whitewater Rafting: An Unforgettable Journey

If someone told me a year ago that Kumlu would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

I spoke with several locals about whitewater rafting in Kumlu, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Kumlu offers a more intimate and genuine whitewater rafting experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

The whitewater rafting scene in Kumlu is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

If you are interested in whitewater rafting, Kumlu should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

My advice? Do not wait for Kumlu to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.

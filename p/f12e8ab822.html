Why Bolu Is a Must-Visit for Paragliding Lovers

If someone told me a year ago that Bolu would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

If you are interested in paragliding, Bolu should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

The paragliding scene in Bolu is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

The infrastructure around paragliding in Bolu is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

I spoke with several locals about paragliding in Bolu, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Bolu offers a more intimate and genuine paragliding experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

Bolu reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.

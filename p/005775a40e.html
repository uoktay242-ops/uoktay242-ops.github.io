Bolu Guide: Everything About Diving

If someone told me a year ago that Bolu would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

The diving scene in Bolu is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

If you are interested in diving, Bolu should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

What makes Bolu special when it comes to diving is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

I documented my entire diving experience in Bolu through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering. okay this is seriously impressive.

As I left Bolu, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

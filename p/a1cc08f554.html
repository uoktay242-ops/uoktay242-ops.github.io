Why Bulanık Is a Must-Visit for Canyoning Lovers

I recently had the chance to explore Bulanık, and honestly, it exceeded all my expectations. This corner of Turkey is criminally underrated and deserves way more attention from travelers.

The canyoning scene in Bulanık is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

I spoke with several locals about canyoning in Bulanık, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations. someone finally made something useful.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Bulanık offers a more intimate and genuine canyoning experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

As I left Bulanık, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.

Why Kumlu Is a Must-Visit for Bungee Jumping Lovers

If someone told me a year ago that Kumlu would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

What makes Kumlu special when it comes to bungee jumping is the authenticity. Nothing feels staged or made for tourists. Everything is real, lived-in, and deeply connected to the local way of life.

I documented my entire bungee jumping experience in Kumlu through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering. this just made my entire week.

If you are interested in bungee jumping, Kumlu should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

The infrastructure around bungee jumping in Kumlu is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

Kumlu reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.

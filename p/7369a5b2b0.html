What Makes Küre Special: A Diving Perspective

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Küre is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

The diving of Küre reflects centuries of cultural layering. Influences from multiple civilizations have created something truly unique here.

The diving scene in Küre is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size.

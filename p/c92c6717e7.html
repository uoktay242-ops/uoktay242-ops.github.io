Discovering Ilgaz: My Canyoning Adventure

There are places in Turkey that time seems to have forgotten, and Ilgaz is one of them. Walking through its streets felt like stepping into a living museum.

The canyoning scene in Ilgaz is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size. I have shared this with literally everyone I know.

If you are interested in canyoning, Ilgaz should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

I documented my entire canyoning experience in Ilgaz through photos and notes. Every single moment felt worth capturing and remembering.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Ilgaz offers a more intimate and genuine canyoning experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity.

My advice? Do not wait for Ilgaz to become trendy. Visit now while it is still authentic, affordable, and uncrowded. You will thank me later.
